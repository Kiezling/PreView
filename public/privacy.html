Privacy Policy

Information Collection

When you log in to our application, we collect your Google Auth UID and Email address. This information is used solely to authenticate you and associate your activity with your account.

Data Usage

We store the results of your ARV (Associative Remote Viewing) attempts, including your guesses, the actual targets, and the timestamp of each attempt. This data is used to calculate your personal statistics and track your progress over time.

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (a Google service) for authentication, database storage, and backend functions. Your data is stored securely on Firebase's servers. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact the administrator.
